Section Subheading 2

Visit Bramhall Park, a beautifully landscaped green space in the suburbs of Greater Manchester. This magnificent park of woodland and trails sits on the grounds of Bramhall Hall, a 14th-century timber-framed hall.

Mam Tor

Section Subheading 2

Visit Mam Tor, a beautiful rural stone-flagged circular walk situated between the Dark Peak and the White Peak of the National Park.

Exercise your mind and body to reach its viewpoint and enjoy one of the most dramatic views in the Peak District.
